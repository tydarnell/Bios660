WTS: For any r.v. X, if $g(x)$ is a convex function, then: $Eg(X)\geq g(EX)$\\
Let $g(x)$ be a convex function\\
Suppose $a+bx$ is a line tangent to $g(x)$ at $x=EX$\\
and $g(x)>a+bx$ except at $x=EX$\\
